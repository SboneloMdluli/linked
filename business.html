Our Vision

Linked is a multi modal mobility integration mobile application. We envision the mobility of the future to be informed by the user experience. We are devoted to the development of data driven analytics solutions to everyday travel that provide first and last mile multi modal solutions to trip planning. We have the goal to generate real time information to commuters across the spectrum both public and private. The future is integrated mobility let’s Get There Together.

Our Mission

Linked is on a mission to empower the urban traveler with the ability to combine all public, shared and on-demand mobility options. The use of travel data to generate comparisons and essential information on commutes is the basis of the urban landscape of the future. A future where the use of public transport creates a greenerenvironment. A future where user generated data informs civic planning around transport and improves urban productivity. Linked acts as the conduit to achieving these goals.

Our users

Students

High School students (aged 13 – 18) & University students (aged 19 – 24) - they live primarily outside urban/city areas where their schools are situated approximately 5- 20 km. They are from low to middle income households which are either child-headed or led by a female guardian (mom, aunt, grandmother). They have access to smartphones and access to one of the top 3 mobile telecommunications providers(MTN, Cell C and Vodacom).

Professionals

Tertiary educated individuals (aged 21 – 40) – they live outside urban/city areas and work for large multi-national corporations or small, medium enterprises (SME). They live primarily in suburbs such as Rosebank, Sandton, Fourways, Randburg and are approximately 5 – 10 km from their place of work.

Aspiring entrepreneurs(aged 18 – 35) – they live in urban areas, suburbs, townships and informal settlements and regularly travel around the province and city of Johannesburg to conduct business deals with investors, suppliers and customers.
